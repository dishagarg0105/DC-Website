Gotham

Gotham is an origin story of the great DC Comics Super-Villains and vigilantes, revealing an entirely
new chapter that has never been told. It follows one cop's rise through a dangerously corrupt city
teetering between good and evil, and chronicles the birth of one of the most popular
super heroes of our time.

Titans

Titans follows the young superheroes of the eponymous team
as they combat evil and other perils. Disbanded when the story
begins, the series sees the team return when the original and new
members reform the Titans. The Titans fight crime throughout
various locations, including Detroit, San Francisco, and Gotham City.

Arrow

Spoiled billionaire playboy Oliver Queen is missing & presumed
dead when his yacht is lost at sea. He returns five years later
a changed man, determined to clean up the city as a
hooded vigilante armed with a bow.

Peacemaker

Peacemaker explores the continuing story of the character that
John Cena reprises in the aftermath of Gunn's 2021 film
"The Suicide Squad" - a compellingly vainglorious man who believes
in peace at any cost, no matter how many people he has to kill to get it.
